# Pricing

@lede Italify can be licensed in two ways. Either per master (unlimited duration), or for a time period (unlimited masters). One credit activates one master, forever. You can decide what option makes more sense for your project.

@overline Per project

## Time passes {#time}

For a sprint across a whole family, a time pass gives you **unlimited masters** – every master in every file, no activation, no counting – for the duration of the pass.

| Pass | Price |
|------|----------|-------|
| One week | 600 € |
| Two weeks | 1 000 € |

Credits and passes combine freely: masters you activate with credits stay activated after a pass ends.

@overline Per master

## Master credits {#credits}

One credit activates one master, **permanently**: run the filter on that master as often as you like, keep editing and re-italifying it forever, including at export. Credits never expire, and top-ups are delivered as a license code by email.

| Package | Credits | Price | Rate/credit | Savings vs. single |

| Large | 16 | 1 700 € | 106 € | 65% |

@overline How it works

## Buying and activating {#how}

1. Get in touch with the package or pass you want: sebastian.carewe<span class="email-protected"></span>
2. You receive your license code by email. [Download the plugin](https://github.com/eweracs/Italify/releases/latest/download/Italify.glyphsPlugin.zip) – the download itself is free, and updates are too: replacing the plugin keeps your license and activated masters in place.
3. Paste the code into the Italify filter dialogue in Glyphs – done. Later top-ups are just another code.
