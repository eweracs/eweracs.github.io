hello about projects

For six semesters, I focused on type design under Luc(as) de Groot, while studying interface design in Potsdam, Germany. I was hooked on type and worked freelance on a multitude of projects, from in-house families and remasters to exclusive typefaces. After studying Italian language and literature for a year, I moved to Porto, Portugal, to work as a font engineer at Schriftlabor. I publish scripts and plugins for Glyphs and various other code on my Github.
Aside from type, I have a broad background in music, with a keen interest in music theory, acoustics and the piano (jazz and classical). In winter, I spend my time speed-skating and on my bike in summer. I can solve a Rubik’s cube in nine seconds, but can’t tell you the point.
Owing to my passion for languages, I am currently studying linguistics in Lille, France.
